About New City College

New City College is one of the leading further education providers in London and the South East. We bring together diverse campuses, staff, and students to deliver high-quality teaching, outstanding support, and strong links to industry and higher education pathways.

#contactGet in touch

Who we are

We are a large, multi-campus college offering further education (FE), adult learning, apprenticeships, and access to higher education. Our teams are dedicated to inclusive, learner-centred practice with a focus on progression—whether to university, employment, or entrepreneurship.

  • Outstanding student support and enrichment
  • Strong employer partnerships and work experience
  • Clear progression routes to higher study and careers

What we offer

Programmes span vocational and academic pathways—from T Levels and A Levels to professional qualifications and short courses for adults. Specialist facilities enable practical learning, and our digital strategy enhances teaching, assessment, and engagement.

Our values

Ambition

We set high expectations for every learner and colleague, celebrating achievement and continuous improvement.

Inclusion

We are proud of our diverse community and committed to equitable access, support, and belonging.

Collaboration

Our community

Students & Alumni

From school leavers to adult returners, our students bring unique experiences and goals. Alumni contribute to mentoring, careers events, and civic initiatives—strengthening connections between education and work.

Staff Development

We invest in professional learning through workshops, masterclasses, and innovation projects to enhance teaching, learning, and assessment across the college.
